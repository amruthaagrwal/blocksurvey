Managerial Effectiveness Self-Review Tool

Purpose & Confidentiality

This tool is designed to measure managerial effectiveness across 11 dimensions. It is intended as an aid for self-review and reflection, not as a scientific or diagnostic measure.

This questionnaire is being conducted solely for academic and research purposes as part of a student project. The information collected will be kept confidential and will be used only in aggregated form for analysis.

Instructions to Participants

  1. There are no right or wrong answers.
  2. Please respond honestly based on your true experiences.
  3. Avoid overthinking your responses, your first instinct is often the most accurate.
  4. Avoid giving socially desirable or ideal answers.
  5. If you have doubts about a question, choose the response closest to the truth.

How to Respond

  1. Read each statement carefully.
  2. Click “Yes” if the statement is true for you.
  3. Click “No” if the statement is not true for you.
  4. Please answer all 110 statements.
  5. If your answer is mostly yes, press Yes. If it is mostly no, press No. If you are confused or unsure, you may choose Yes.
